Riesling

Quick answer

Riesling ranges from bone-dry to lusciously sweet, always with piercing acidity. Off-dry styles tame spice; dry styles lift fatty fish and aromatic Asian dishes.

Aromatic white pairing uses acidity and optional sweetness to balance heat, umami, and rich sauces.

FAQ

Is all Riesling sweet?

No. Many are dry (Trocken); sweetness varies by region and style.

Why does Riesling pair with spicy food?

Off-dry fruit and acidity soften heat better than high-alcohol dry reds.

What is petrol in Riesling?

A mineral note from TDN in aged Riesling — prized, not a fault.
