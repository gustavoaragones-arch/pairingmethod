Wine Region

Rheingau

Germany · Appellation Region

The Rheingau is Germany's historic Riesling center — south-facing slopes along the Rhine producing dry Spätlese and structured Grosses Gewächs.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Cool continental, sheltered
  • Soils: slate, loess, quartzite
  • Elevation: 80–200m

Parent region
