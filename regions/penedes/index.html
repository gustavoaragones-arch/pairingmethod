Wine Region

Penedès

Spain · Appellation Region

Penedès is Cava's heartland — traditional-method sparkling from Macabeo, Xarel·lo, and Parellada on rolling hills between Barcelona and the Mediterranean.

Also known as: Penedès (Spain)

Geographic context

  • Climate: Mediterranean
  • Soils: limestone, clay, sand
  • Elevation: 200–800m

Parent region
