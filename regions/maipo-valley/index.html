Wine Region

Maipo Valley

Chile · Appellation Region

Maipo Valley near Santiago is Chile's Cabernet Sauvignon benchmark — Andes-cooled vineyards producing structured cassis and minty reds.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Mediterranean with Andes cooling
  • Soils: alluvial, gravel, clay
  • Elevation: 400–800m
