Wine Region

New Zealand

New Zealand · Country

New Zealand is synonymous with zesty Sauvignon Blanc from Marlborough and elegant Pinot Noir from Central Otago and Martinborough. Cool maritime climate preserves vibrant acidity and pure fruit.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Cool maritime
  • Soils: alluvial, gravel, clay, schist
  • Elevation: Sea level to 400m
