Wine Region

Piedmont

Italy · Appellation Region

Piedmont is Nebbiolo's kingdom — Barolo and Barbaresco on fog-shrouded hills, plus Moscato d'Asti and Barbera. High acidity and firm tannin define food-centric Italian classics.

Also known as: Piemonte

Geographic context

  • Climate: Continental with fog
  • Soils: limestone, marl, clay, sand
  • Elevation: 200–500m

FAQ

Barolo vs Barbaresco?
Both are Nebbiolo; Barolo is fuller and longer-aging; Barbaresco is earlier maturing and often more perfumed.
