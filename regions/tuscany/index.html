Wine Region

Tuscany

Italy · Appellation Region

Tuscany is Sangiovese territory — Chianti, Brunello di Montalcino, and Super Tuscans on galestro and clay. High acidity and savory cherry define Italy's most iconic food wines.

Also known as: Toscana

Geographic context

  • Climate: Mediterranean
  • Soils: galestro, clay, limestone, sandstone
  • Elevation: 150–600m

Parent region

FAQ

Chianti vs Brunello?
Both Sangiovese — Brunello uses a specific clone (Sangiovese Grosso) with stricter aging.
