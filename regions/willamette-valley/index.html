Wine Region

Willamette Valley

United States · AVA

Willamette Valley is Oregon's Pinot Noir heartland — cool, rainy climate on volcanic and sedimentary soils producing Burgundian-style elegance.

Also known as: Oregon, Willamette Valley (Oregon)

Geographic context

  • Climate: Cool maritime
  • Soils: volcanic, sedimentary, clay, loess
  • Elevation: 50–300m
