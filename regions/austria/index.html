Wine Region

Austria

Austria · Country

Austria specializes in precise, dry whites — Grüner Veltliner and Riesling from terraced Danube valleys — with a strict quality system and food-friendly acidity.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Cool continental
  • Soils: loess, gneiss, limestone
  • Elevation: 200–400m on river terraces

Typical grape varieties

  • Riesling
  • Pinot Noir
  • Sauvignon Blanc

Typical wine styles

  • Riesling
  • Pinot Grigio

Typical descriptors

  • Lime
  • Peach
  • Black pepper
  • Crisp
  • Minerality
  • Elegant

Food pairings

  • Viennese cuisine and schnitzel
