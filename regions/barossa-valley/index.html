Wine Region

Barossa Valley

Australia · Geographic Indication

Barossa Valley is Shiraz heartland — old-vine, full-bodied, peppery reds from warm valley floor and elevated Eden Valley fringe.

Also known as: Barossa Valley (Australia)

Geographic context

  • Climate: Warm continental
  • Soils: clay, sand, ironstone, limestone
  • Elevation: 200–400m

FAQ

Barossa vs Eden Valley?
Barossa floor is warmer Shiraz; Eden Valley is cooler, higher, and more peppery.
