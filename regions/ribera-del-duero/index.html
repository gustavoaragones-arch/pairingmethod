Wine Region

Ribera del Duero

Spain · Appellation Region

Ribera del Duero produces powerful Tempranillo (Tinto Fino) on high plateau — darker fruit, firmer tannin, and longer aging potential than many Rioja styles.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Continental, extreme diurnal shift
  • Soils: limestone, clay, alluvial
  • Elevation: 750–900m
