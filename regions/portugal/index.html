Wine Region

Portugal

Portugal · Country

Portugal is defined by indigenous grapes and Atlantic influence — Port from the Douro, Vinho Verde's spritz, and increasingly serious table wines. Fortified traditions coexist with modern dry styles.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Maritime Atlantic to hot interior
  • Soils: schist, granite, clay, volcanic
  • Elevation: Sea level to 700m in Douro

FAQ

What is Port wine?
Fortified wine from the Douro Valley — fermentation stopped with grape spirit to preserve sweetness.
