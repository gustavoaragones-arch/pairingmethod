Wine Region

Veneto

Italy · Appellation Region

Veneto produces Prosecco, Pinot Grigio, and Amarone — from sparkling Glera in the hills to rich appassimento reds in Valpolicella.

Also known as: Veneto and Friuli

Geographic context

  • Climate: Continental to sub-Mediterranean
  • Soils: volcanic, limestone, alluvial, clay
  • Elevation: 50–500m
