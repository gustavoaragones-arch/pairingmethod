Wine Region

Clare Valley

Australia · Geographic Indication

Clare Valley Riesling is Australia's benchmark dry white — lime, slate, and piercing acidity from a warm days/cool nights valley north of Adelaide.

Also known as: Australia (Clare Valley), Clare Valley (Australia)

Geographic context

  • Climate: Warm days, cool nights
  • Soils: slate, limestone, clay, red loam
  • Elevation: 300–500m

FAQ

Is Clare Valley Riesling dry?
Yes — Australian Clare Riesling is typically bone dry with intense lime and slate notes.
