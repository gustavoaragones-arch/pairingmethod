Wine Region

Provence

France · Appellation Region

Provence defines pale, dry rosé — Grenache, Cinsault, and Syrah on limestone hills with Mediterranean herb influence. Côtes de Provence and Bandol anchor the style.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Mediterranean
  • Soils: limestone, clay, schist
  • Elevation: 0–400m

FAQ

Why is Provence rosé so pale?
Short skin contact and gentle pressing limit color while preserving freshness.
