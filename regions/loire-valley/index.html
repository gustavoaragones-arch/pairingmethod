Loire Valley

France · Appellation Region

The Loire spans Sauvignon Blanc in Sancerre, Chenin Blanc in Vouvray, and Cabernet Franc in Chinon — all marked by vibrant acidity and food versatility across a long, cool river valley.

Also known as: Loire

Geographic context

  • Climate: Cool maritime to continental
  • Soils: limestone, flint, tuffeau, gravel
  • Elevation: 0–200m
