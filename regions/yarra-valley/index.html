Wine Region

Yarra Valley

Australia · Geographic Indication

The Yarra Valley is Victoria's cool-climate hub — Pinot Noir and Chardonnay with finesse, plus sparkling wines from elevated sites near Melbourne.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Cool maritime
  • Soils: volcanic, clay, sandstone
  • Elevation: 50–400m
