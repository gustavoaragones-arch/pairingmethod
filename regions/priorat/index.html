Wine Region

Priorat

Spain · Appellation Region

Priorat is rugged Garnacha and Cariñena on steep llicorella slate — concentrated, mineral-driven reds with depth and intensity rivaling top global regions.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Mediterranean continental
  • Soils: slate, llicorella, quartzite
  • Elevation: 100–700m on steep terraces

Parent region

Spain

FAQ

What is llicorella?
Priorat's distinctive slate-and-quartz soil that stresses vines and concentrates flavor.
