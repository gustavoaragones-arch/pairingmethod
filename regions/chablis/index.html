Wine Region

Chablis

France · Subregion

Chablis is steely, unoaked Chardonnay on Kimmeridgian limestone — green apple, citrus, and oyster-shell minerality. A benchmark for lean, food-friendly white Burgundy.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Cool continental
  • Soils: limestone, clay, kimmeridgian
  • Elevation: 150–250m

FAQ

Is Chablis oaked?
Traditionally no — stainless or neutral vessel preserves purity and minerality.
