Wine Region

Sonoma

United States · AVA

Sonoma County spans cool Russian River Pinot Noir, Alexander Valley Cabernet, and Dry Creek Zinfandel — diverse microclimates in one county.

Also known as: Sonoma (USA), Sonoma County

Geographic context

  • Climate: Maritime to warm inland
  • Soils: volcanic, alluvial, clay, sand
  • Elevation: 0–700m

Sonoma vs Napa?
Sonoma is more diverse and often cooler; Napa is more Cabernet-focused and warmer overall.
