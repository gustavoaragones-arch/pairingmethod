Wine Region

Napa Valley

United States · AVA

Napa Valley is California's premium Cabernet Sauvignon capital — warm valley floor and benchland vineyards producing cassis, cedar, and structured reds with global recognition.

Also known as: Napa Valley (USA)

Geographic context

  • Climate: Warm Mediterranean
  • Soils: volcanic, alluvial, clay, gravel
  • Elevation: 0–600m
