Wine Region

Rhône Valley

France · Appellation Region

The Rhône splits into peppery Northern Syrah (Hermitage, Côte-Rôtie) and warm Southern blends (Châteauneuf-du-Pape). Condrieu showcases Viognier; the south excels at Grenache-based GSM wines.

Also known as: Rhône, Northern Rhône, Southern Rhône

Geographic context

  • Climate: Continental north; Mediterranean south
  • Soils: granite, schist, galets, clay
  • Elevation: 100–500m

FAQ

Northern vs Southern Rhône?
North is Syrah-focused and peppery; south is Grenache blends, warmer and riper.
