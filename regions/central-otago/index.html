Wine Region

Central Otago

New Zealand · Geographic Indication

Central Otago is the world's southernmost Pinot Noir region — continental climate, schist soils, and vivid cherry fruit with firm structure.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Continental, semi-arid
  • Soils: schist, gravel, clay, silt
  • Elevation: 200–450m

What makes Central Otago Pinot unique?
Continental climate and schist soils produce riper fruit with firm acidity and spice.
