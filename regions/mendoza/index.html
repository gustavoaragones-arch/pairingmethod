Wine Region

Mendoza

Argentina · Appellation Region

Mendoza accounts for most Argentine wine — high-altitude Malbec from Luján de Cuyo and Uco Valley with plush fruit, violet, and soft tannin.

Also known as: Mendoza (Argentina)

Geographic context

  • Climate: High-altitude desert
  • Soils: alluvial, sand, clay
  • Elevation: 600–1,500m

Parent region
