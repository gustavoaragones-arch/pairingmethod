Wine Region

Bordeaux

France · Appellation Region

Bordeaux is the benchmark for Cabernet-Merlot blends — Left Bank gravel cassis and structure, Right Bank Merlot plushness. Age-worthy, food-driven reds define the global fine wine market.

Geographic context

  • Climate: Maritime temperate
  • Soils: gravel, clay, limestone
  • Elevation: 0–100m

FAQ

Left Bank vs Right Bank Bordeaux?
Left Bank is Cabernet-dominant on gravel; Right Bank is Merlot-dominant on clay-limestone.
