Wine Region

Coonawarra

Australia · Geographic Indication

Coonawarra's terra rossa strip over limestone produces structured Cabernet Sauvignon — cassis, mint, and firm tannin with exceptional aging.

Also known as: Coonawarra (Australia)

Geographic context

  • Climate: Cool maritime
  • Soils: terra rossa, limestone, clay
  • Elevation: 20–60m
