Wine pairing explained simply

Wine pairing works by balancing acidity, tannin, body, and flavor intensity. For example, high-tannin wines like Cabernet Sauvignon pair well with fatty meats such as steak because tannins bind to protein and fat, softening the wine’s structure on the palate.
