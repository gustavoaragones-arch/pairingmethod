Why These Pairings Work

Acidity

High acidity cuts richness and keeps each bite balanced with your dish.

Tannin

Tannin softens against protein and fat—the reason bold reds work with steak.

Body

Matching body to dish weight prevents the wine from overpowering the plate.

Flavor Intensity

Aligned intensity lets delicate dishes stay delicate and bold flavors stay in harmony.
