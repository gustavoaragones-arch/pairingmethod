Aromatic White Wine Service

Glassware

Aromatic whites like Gewürztraminer need a glass with a slightly wider opening and moderate chill to amplify lychee, rose, and spice without residual sugar cloying. Serve chilled, not ice-cold, in a shape that collects perfume.

Also known as: Gewürztraminer service, Perfumed white protocol

FAQ

Why does Gewürztraminer need a wider glass?
The intense perfume benefits from a bowl that collects volatile aromatics — a standard narrow white glass can underdeliver.
