Wine Serving

Dessert Wine Glass

Glassware

A smaller glass with a slight taper concentrates sweetness, alcohol, and nutty complexity for Port, Madeira, and Moscato. The reduced pour size keeps fortified and dessert wines balanced on the palate.

Also known as: Small port glass, Fortified wine glass

FAQ

How much do you pour in a dessert wine glass?
Typically 2–3 ounces — about half a standard wine pour — for fortified and sweet wines.
