Short-Term Aging

Cellaring

Short-term aging (roughly 2–5 years) suits entry Chardonnay, Cava Reserva, Gewürztraminer, Grenache, and Zinfandel — modest gains in integration without long-term commitment.

Also known as: 2–5 years, Near-term cellaring

FAQ

What conditions for short-term storage?
Cool (55°F), dark, humid, and vibration-free — a closet or wine fridge works; no need for a full cellar.
