Wine Serving

Fortified Wine Service

Serving Temperature

Fortified wines need tailored service — Fino Sherry and Manzanilla chilled; Oloroso and Port slightly cool to cellar warm; Madeira flexible by sweetness. Small pours, appropriate glassware, and freshness for biologically aged styles are essential.

Also known as: Port and Sherry service, Fortified protocol

FAQ

Should Port be served warm like red wine?
Ruby and Vintage Port do well at cool room or cellar temperature — not summer-warm; Tawny can take a slight chill.
