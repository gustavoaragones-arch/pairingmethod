1 Hour Decant

Decanting

One hour in a decanter opens young, structured reds — Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Sangiovese Brunello, and Vintage Port. Tannins polymerize perceptibly, fruit integrates, and youthful reduction clears.

Also known as: One hour aeration, 60 minute decant

FAQ

Can you decant too long at one hour?
Rarely for young tannic reds; delicate or very old wines may fade — taste periodically.
