Wine Serving

Under-Decanted

Serving Mistake

Under-decanting leaves young, tannic reds tight and reductive — closed fruit, aggressive tannin, and muted aroma. Common with young Cabernet, Nebbiolo, and Syrah opened just before pouring.

Also known as: Insufficient aeration, Closed wine

Recommended for wine styles

  • Cabernet Sauvignon
  • Nebbiolo
  • Syrah / Shiraz
  • Sangiovese

Typical grape varieties

  • Cabernet Sauvignon

Related descriptors

  • Firm
  • Austere
  • Closed

Related regions

  • Bordeaux
  • Barolo

FAQ

How do you fix under-decanted wine?
Pour into a decanter or large glass and wait — double decanting accelerates opening for stubborn bottles.
