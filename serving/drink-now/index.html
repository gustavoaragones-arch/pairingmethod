Wine Serving

Drink Now

Cellaring

Drink-now wines are made for immediate freshness — most Pinot Grigio, Sauvignon Blanc, rosé, Prosecco, Moscato, and young Albariño. Cellaring adds little; the appeal is primary fruit and vibrancy.

Also known as: Enjoy young, No cellaring needed

FAQ

Can drink-now wines age at all?
A year or two is fine for many; beyond that, fruit and acidity typically fade without compensating complexity.
