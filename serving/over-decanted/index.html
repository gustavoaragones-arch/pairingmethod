Wine Serving

Over-Decanted

Serving Mistake

Over-decanting exposes wine to excessive oxygen — fragile older bottles fade within minutes, aromatic whites flatten, and sparklers are ruined entirely. Young tannic reds tolerate more; aged wines need caution.

Also known as: Too much aeration, Oxidized from decanting

Recommended for wine styles

  • Pinot Noir
  • Nebbiolo
  • Chardonnay
  • Champagne

Typical grape varieties

  • Pinot Noir

Related descriptors

  • Flat
  • Oxidized
  • Tired

FAQ

Can you save an over-decanted wine?
Sometimes recorking and chilling slows further decline, but lost aromatics rarely return.
