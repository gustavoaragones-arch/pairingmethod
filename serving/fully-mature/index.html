Wine Serving

Fully Mature

Aging Potential

Fully mature wines are at or near the end of their optimal arc — tertiary notes dominate, fruit fades, and texture thins. Handle gently: shorter decants, cooler service, and prompt consumption once opened.

Also known as: Late phase, Declining plateau

FAQ

Is fully mature the same as over the hill?
Not always — some styles like Tawny Port and Madeira are designed to taste best fully mature.
