Wine Serving

Wrong Glass

Serving Mistake

Wrong glassware undermines the wine — Champagne in a coupe loses bubbles, Pinot Noir in a small glass mutes aroma, and fortified wine in a large bowl exaggerates alcohol heat. Glass shape directs wine to palate zones and concentrates aromatics.

Also known as: Incorrect glassware, Mismatched glass

Recommended for wine styles

  • Pinot Noir
  • Cabernet Sauvignon
  • Port

  • Chardonnay

Related descriptors

FAQ

Does glassware really matter?
For fine wine, yes — shape affects aroma intensity, bubble retention, and perceived tannin more than most expect.
