Wine Serving

White Wine Glass

Glassware

The narrower white wine glass preserves temperature and concentrates citrus, stone fruit, and floral aromatics. Standard choice for Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Grigio, Albariño, Chenin Blanc, rosé, and Sherry.

Also known as: Standard white glass, Smaller bowl glass

FAQ

Why are white wine glasses smaller?
Smaller bowls keep wine colder longer and funnel delicate aromatics to the nose.
