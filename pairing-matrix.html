Wine Pairing Matrix

Quick answer

The matrix is a printable grid of pairing scores between sample dishes and five grape styles. Higher numbers mean stronger structured matches from the same logic as the pairing engine. Use dish guides for sauces and preparation detail the grid cannot show.

Wine pairing works by balancing intensity, acidity, tannin, fat, and texture between food and wine.
