About Pairing Method

Quick answer

Pairing Method publishes structured wine pairing education: why matches work, not only what to pour. The site is chef-forward—sauces and technique matter. Founder: Gustavo Aragones; intent is clarity and repeatable rules for cooks and hosts.

Wine pairing works by balancing intensity, acidity, tannin, fat, and texture between food and wine.

Mission

Pairing Method exists to make wine and food pairing clear, structured, and repeatable. We give you the logic behind great pairings so you can apply it to any dish — not just a list of suggestions.

Structured Pairing Philosophy

We focus on why pairings work: weight, tannin, acidity, sweetness, and flavor bridges. By explaining the rules (and when to break them), we help you build intuition that works in the kitchen and at the table. No guesswork, no fluff.

Chef-Forward

Our content is built for people who cook and care about what’s on the plate. We consider preparation, sauces, and technique — not just the main ingredient — so our recommendations hold up in real cooking and dining contexts.

This site provides structured wine pairing guidance for informational purposes only. Recommendations are based on general culinary principles and may vary depending on preparation, ingredients, and personal preference.
