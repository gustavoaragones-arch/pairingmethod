About Pairing Method

Pairing Method is a structured wine pairing system built on culinary principles. Instead of relying on generic recommendations, it analyzes how food components — such as protein, fat, acidity, and preparation — interact with wine structure.

The platform is designed as a decision-support tool for everyday cooking, helping users choose the right wine based on how a dish is prepared, not just what it is.

How It Works

The system evaluates compatibility using a multi-factor model derived from classic pairing principles:

  • Intensity alignment
  • Acidity and fat balance
  • Tannin and protein interaction
  • Sweetness and spice balance
